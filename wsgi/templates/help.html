How to conduct the test

  1. Measure your weight.
  2. Warm up.
  3. Take an easy, steady pace, 1 mile jog, timing how long it takes.
  4. Record your heart rate (bpm) immediately after the jog.

To interpret your VO2max result, look at the chart data for you gender and age group.
